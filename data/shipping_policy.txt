**Shipping Policy**

**1. Domestic Shipping**
We offer free standard shipping (3-5 business days) on all domestic orders over $50.
Expedited shipping (2-day) is available for a flat rate of $15.
Overnight shipping is available for $25.

**2. International Shipping**
We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

**Tiers:**
- **Tier 1 (North America - Non-Domestic):** 5-7 business days.
- **Tier 2 (Europe & Asia):** 7-14 business days.
- **Tier 3 (Rest of World):** 14-21 business days.

**3. Customs Duties and Taxes**
For international orders, **the customer is responsible for all customs duties, import taxes, and brokerage fees**. These are not included in the shipping price and are collected by the carrier upon delivery.
*Note:* Refusing to pay customs duties will result in the package being abandoned or returned. Original shipping charges will not be refunded in such cases.

**4. Order Processing**
Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST are shipped the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST are shipped the next business day.
