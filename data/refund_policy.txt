**Refund Policy**

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024

**1. Standard Refund Window**
All physical hardware products (laptops, monitors, accessories) are eligible for a full refund within **30 days** of the purchase date. The item must be in its original condition, unopened or like-new, with all original packaging and accessories included.

**2. Return Process**
To initiate a return, log into your account portal and navigate to "Order History". Select the item you wish to return and click "Request Refund". A shipping label will be generated for you.

**3. Software Exceptions**
Software licenses, downloadable content, and subscription services are **non-refundable** once the license key has been revealed or the software has been downloaded/activated.
*Exception:* If the software is proven to be defective or incompatible with the specified operating system requirements within 7 days of purchase, a support ticket may be raised for a potential exception.

**4. Restocking Fees**
Returns made after the 30-day window (if approved by management) or items returned with missing components may be subject to a **15% restocking fee**.

**5. Processing Time**
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse.
